Privacy Policy

How MediBridge collects, uses, protects, and shares patient and visitor information.

Last updated: June 27, 2026

1. Who We Are

MediBridge is a healthcare portal project by lawalolamide48-cell. It helps patients explore departments, book appointments, manage health information, and ask general medical questions through AI support.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the information you enter into the platform, including your name, patient ID, email address, phone number, appointment details, department selections, messages, and AI support questions.

For security, MediBridge also generates short-lived verification codes when you request account activation or password recovery.

3. How We Use Information

  • To create, verify, and protect patient accounts.
  • To send OTP verification codes by email or SMS.
  • To help patients browse departments and book appointments.
  • To provide general AI health guidance and support responses.
  • To improve safety, reliability, and user experience.

4. Medical Information

MediBridge may display or process health-related information you provide. The AI assistant is for general information only and does not provide diagnosis, treatment, prescriptions, emergency response, or a replacement for professional medical advice.

5. OTP, Email, and SMS Delivery

When you request verification, MediBridge generates a temporary OTP and may send it through configured email and SMS providers. OTP codes expire after 5 minutes and are stored as hashes, not plain text.

6. Sharing Information

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared with service providers that help deliver platform features, such as email, SMS, hosting, security, or appointment communication providers.

7. Data Security

MediBridge uses practical safeguards such as OTP expiry, attempt limits, and server-side verification. A production deployment should also include encrypted databases, secure sessions, password hashing, audit logs, access controls, and regular security review.

8. Your Choices

You may choose not to provide certain information, but some features may not work without it. You may also request correction or deletion of your information from the project owner or platform administrator.

9. Children

Pediatric care features are intended to be used by parents, guardians, or authorized caregivers. Children should not create accounts without appropriate permission.

10. Contact

For privacy questions about this project, contact the MediBridge project owner: lawalolamide48-cell.

This policy is written for a student/demo healthcare project and should be reviewed by a qualified legal professional before real clinical deployment.
